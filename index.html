Hometown is a place of departure. A place that is always missed, a place that stands in the name of childhood memories, the past, nostalgic stories, and memories of times past. Hometown is a place to return to, a place whose spirit is able to attract us to always long to go home, as if it were a shoulder to lean on that we always want to turn to. The smell of the rain, the leaves, the soil, the water, the air, feels real even if only in the memory in your head. Humans can survive anywhere, but there will always be a place where our souls have become a part of it. To that part of the soul, we want to visit it even if only once a year.
